About Us

A school built around the child

Founded in 2019, Sri Ramana Vidyalaya is a co-educational primary school committed to holistic education. We believe learning should be joyful, purposeful, and rooted in strong values.

Our spacious green campus, small class sizes, and dedicated faculty create an environment where children feel safe to ask questions, take risks, and grow.

  • 🎯 Strong academic foundation
  • 🤝 Values & character first
  • 🎨 Arts, sports & STEM for all
  • 🌱 Small classes, individual care

Our Vision

To nurture confident, compassionate, and responsible lifelong learners who excel academically, uphold strong moral values, and contribute positively to society with integrity, wisdom, and respect.

Our Mission

01

To provide a safe, inclusive, and stimulating learning environment where every child can realize their full potential.

02

To promote academic excellence through innovative and learner-centered teaching practices.

03

To instill discipline, honesty, empathy, respect, and social responsibility in every student.

04

To encourage creativity, critical thinking, communication, and collaboration for success in a changing world.

05

To foster strong partnerships among students, parents, teachers, and the community for holistic development.

06

To inspire every learner to become a responsible global citizen rooted in Indian culture and universal human values.

Admissions 2026–27

Join the Sri Ramana Vidyalaya family

Admissions are now open for all grades. The process is simple, and our team is here to help you at every step.

  1. Enquire — Fill the form and we'll reach out within 2 working days.
  2. Visit — Tour the campus and meet our faculty.
  3. Apply — Submit documents and complete a friendly interaction.
  4. Welcome — Confirm your seat and join orientation.

📄 Documents needed: birth certificate, previous report card, transfer certificate (Grade 2+), and passport photos.
